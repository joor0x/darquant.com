01The evolution loop

A genetic algorithm that runs your strategy book like a species.

Every cycle, code-level strategies are crossed and mutated, stress-tested out-of-sample, and ranked by multi-objective fitness. Weak lineages die. Strong ones reproduce and graduate to live capital.

02The Arena

Strategies don't get deployed. They earn it.

The Arena is a continuous tournament. Capital is normalized across competitors, exposure is aggregated, and a deterministic curator retires underperformers with a full soft-delete audit trail. No black-box deletions — every promotion and culling is logged.

Circuit breakers, slippage tracking and collision detection keep the live book honest. Metrics stream to Prometheus & Grafana in real time.
